## When to promote something into this layer

- You've seen the same correction from Raleigh/Tim more than once.
- A pattern surfaced in a campaign wrap-up that applies across other campaigns.
- A HubSpot API gotcha bit us more than once and deserves documentation.

## When to demote something out of this layer

- The pattern turned out to be wrong after more data.
- The pattern is now codified in operations/ or reference/ and doesn't need its own playbook entry.
- The pattern became obvious and doesn't need saying.
