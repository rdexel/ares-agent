# Socratics — Positioning

## One line

Institutional-grade financial models in under 10 minutes, built specifically for private capital markets.

## What we do

Socratics takes raw, messy financial data and turns it into structured, audit-ready 3-statement models. Formula-linked, fully traceable, every assumption visible. Purpose-built for PE and IB teams — not a general-purpose AI tool adapted for finance.

## The pain we fix

Senior people doing work that shouldn't reach their desk — normalizing messy financials, rebuilding models from scratch, chasing numbers that don't tie out. **The variance and time loss happen upstream, before modeling starts.** We fix that intake layer.

## Core value prop (use in every cold email)

Institutional financial models in under 10 minutes, built specifically for private capital markets. Speed and accuracy.

## Differentiator vs. generic AI tools

Produces formula-linked models with full audit trails. Not black-box outputs — every number is traceable. Purpose-built for how PE and IB teams actually work.

## ICP

**Target firms:**
- Private equity funds (LMM to upper middle market)
- Investment banks (sell-side advisory, especially middle market)
- Private capital funds (credit, growth equity, special situations)

**Target personas inside the firm:**
- Deal team: Analysts, Associates, Senior Associates, VPs, Principals
- Senior decision-makers: Directors, MDs, Partners
- Operating partners (for firm-wide rollouts)

**Filter out (NOT ICP):**
- Assistants, EAs, Chief of Staff
- Finance/accounting/controllers (internal ops, not deal side)
- Marketing, HR, IR, compliance, legal
- Membership, education, industry affairs
- Operations roles not tied to deal work

## Scale proof point

200+ investment banks and private capital funds use Socratics.
